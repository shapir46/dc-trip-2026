🥪 Lunch Stop: Shapiro's Delicatessen — Indianapolis, IN
A legendary deli open since 1905, just off I-70/65 on the south edge of downtown Indianapolis. Cafeteria-style so the line moves fast — grab a tray and load up. Famous for mile-high corned beef and pastrami on rye, matzo ball soup, house-made rye bread, and an incredible bakery case. A beloved Indiana institution and a perfect road trip meal for a big, hungry family. 808 S Meridian St, Indianapolis · ~$15–20/person
📍 ~4.5 hrs from DC · ~3.5 hrs from Birmingham
Open Mon–Fri 10 AM–7:30 PM · Sat–Sun 9 AM–7:30 PM. Kids can pick what they want cafeteria-style. Save room for a giant brownie or black-and-white cookie from the bakery — they're legendary.
🌐 Website ⭐ Yelp 🗺 Route
